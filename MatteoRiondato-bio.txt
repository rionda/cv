Matteo Riondato is a research scientist at Two Sigma Investments and an adjunct
assistant professor at Brown University. In January 2019 he will join Amherst
College as an assistant professor. His research focus is in algorithms for
knowledge discovery: he develops methods to analyze rich datasets, including
graphs and time series, as fast as possible and in a statistically sound way.
His works received the best-of-conference awards at the 2014 SIAM International
Conference on Data Mining (SDM), the 2016 ACM SIGKDD International Conference on
Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining (KDD), and the 2018 IEEE International
Conference on Data Mining. He tweets @teorionda and lives at http://matteo.rionda.to .
